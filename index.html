About Me

My name is Christopher Chaiban and I really do enjoy playing video games. I usually stick to a certain genre of games, but sometimes I do branch out and try new things. Sometimes I play them out of enjoyment, other times I play to improve and become better. In the end, I believe that the medium of video games was probably the greatest invention to come about and I am glad to be born in such a time. There are so many different games that it can become daunting at points, but I love it nonetheless.

Review Methodology

For my reviews, I delve into 4 different aspects of each game, Gameplay, Story, Visuals and Audio, and Performance. I will then give a final score out of 10 based on my opinion of the game. Obviously, I will try to be unbiased regarding the final verdict, but it is still my own review of the game so take everything with a grain of salt. In the end, the point of these reviews is to give a general idea of the game and one of the many opinions people will hold about that game, that is the beauty of the internet and voicing our own opinions.

Why I Review Games

There are two main reasons why I review games, one is purely for myself and getting a greater understanding about how I actually feel about the games I play, the other is to be able to share my thoughts and opinions with others as I believe that what I have to say can be somewhat valuable to anyone who has hesitation in a playing a certain game.
